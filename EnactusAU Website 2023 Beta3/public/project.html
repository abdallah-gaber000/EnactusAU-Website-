AZZAM

In co-operation with Faculty of Agriculture of Assiut University, we have grown barley plant to replace the usual fodder, the clover, as barely requires less water and agriculture space, in an attempt to improve livestock production.

TOUBA

It was implemented in the village of Jahdam in Assiut, where there is a cement brick factory that stopped its activity years ago. The team contributed to reviving the factory again by using an innovative way to produce cement bricks , providing job opportunities for six of the villagers and working on marketing the product.

WOODIE

We used palm fronds to produce cheaper, finer and stronger block boards, which in turn reduced the amount of palm fronds burned every year in Assiut, and offered high quality boards at low price.
